FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Uprint?
Uprint is a cloud-based printing solution in Bangladesh. You upload your document from your phone or computer, choose print settings, get an OTP, and collect the printout from a nearby Uprint kiosk — without needing a pendrive or touching a shop PC.
Can I upload from my phone?
Yes. Uprint is designed to work from your phone browser. Upload files from your gallery, files app, or cloud storage. There's also a dedicated mobile app on the Google Play Store if you prefer.
Do I need to visit the kiosk first?
No. The upload, settings, and confirmation all happen online from your own device first. You only visit the kiosk at the end — to press your OTP and collect the printout.
What file types can I print?
Uprint supports common document formats including PDF, Word (.docx), and image files (JPG, PNG). PDF is recommended for the most accurate output — especially for admit cards, forms, and formatted documents.
How does the OTP pickup work?
After confirming your print job, Uprint gives you a unique OTP code. Visit any Uprint kiosk, press your OTP on the kiosk screen, and it releases your printout. No need to speak to anyone or explain your job.
Do I need a pendrive?
No — that's the whole point of Uprint. You upload from your own device directly. No physical storage is required at any step of the process.
How much does printing cost?
Uprint shows you the exact cost estimate before you confirm any print job — based on your chosen settings (pages, copies, color/B&W). Online payment is also supported so you can pay before reaching the kiosk.
